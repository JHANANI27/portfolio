ABOUT ME

Evelynn is K/DA'S lead vocalist ,weilding a hypnotic stage presence like no other .Never one to stay in the shadows.She relishes the crowd's reactions to her masterfully crafted image.Co-founder of K/DA alongside her counterpart Ahri,evelynn edges the group towards their most thrilling creative risks.
